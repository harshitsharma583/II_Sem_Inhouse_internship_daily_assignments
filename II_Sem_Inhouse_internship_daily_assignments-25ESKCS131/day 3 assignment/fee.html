Admission Process

  • Admission to B.Tech and M.Tech courses is based on the JEE Main examination conducted by NTA.
  • For MBA admissions, candidates are required to appear for CAT or any equivalent management entrance examination.
  • Students should check the official SKIT website for admission procedures, eligibility criteria and important dates.
  • Applicants should submit their applications within the specified deadlines.
  • Student must get more than or equal to 75% marks in 12th board exam.

Fees Structure

Course Fees per Year
B.Tech 1,00,000 INR
M.Tech 1,20,000 INR
MBA 1,50,000 INR

Payment Options

  • Students can pay the fees in installments as per the institute policy.
  • Online payment options are available for convenient transactions.
  • Scholarship opportunities are provided to meritorious students.
